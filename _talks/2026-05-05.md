[Public lecture](https://www.durham.ac.uk/departments/academic/physics/about-us/events/public-lecture-cosmos-to-clinic-towards-personalised-cancer-care-inspired-by-astrophysics/) on the application of statistical techniques from astrophysics and cosmology to oncology, i.e. personalised cancer care, hosted by the [Institute for Computational Cosmology, Durham University](https://icc.dur.ac.uk/) as part of the [Next Frontier in Medical Data 2026 colloquium](https://theory-to-therapy.github.io/index.html). The lecture was presented together with cancer researchers and medical practicitioners [Dr Navita Somaiah](https://www.icr.ac.uk/research-and-discoveries/find-a-researcher/test-researcher-profile-detail/dr-navita-somaiah) and [Dr Uzma Asghar](https://uk.linkedin.com/in/uzma-asghar-01a8b244).
